About the App

English Word Reminder App is designed for English learners looking to expand their vocabulary through daily notifications and fun exercises. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced learner, our app personalizes your learning experience with tailored word lists and quizzes.

Features

Daily Reminders

Receive notifications at selected times to learn new words each day.

Personalized Word Lists

Create, manage, and customize your word lists based on your level or interests.

Interactive Quizzes

Practice what you learn with multiple-choice, fill-in-the-blank, and more.

Progress Tracking

Monitor your learning and stay motivated by unlocking achievements over time.
